Sugar Swoop
How to play

Welcome to SugarSwoop, a thrilling single-player game where you navigate a whimsical world filled with candy-themed challenges! Inspired by the vibrant aesthetics of Wreck-It Ralph's Sugar Rush, this game combines engaging gameplay with delightful visuals to create an exciting gaming experience.

Objective

Dodge or shoot the enemies that come from the bottom of the screen. Collect sweets for points and hearts to stay in the game longer.

Movement

Move your character using the arrow keys or WASD.

Shooting

Press the spacebar to shoot peppermint bullets.

Enemies

Candy Bandits

Slows you down
Removes a life if hit
Shoot for 5 points

Cotton Candy Cloud

Ends the game!
Shoot for 20 points

Candy Bolts

Significantly slows you down
Deducts 10 points if hit
Shoot for 10 points

Power-ups

Lollipop Tokens

Collect or shoot for 5 points

Candy Hearts

Collect or shoot for an extra life

Scoring

Gain points by collecting sweets. Stay in the game longer by collecting hearts for extra lives.
